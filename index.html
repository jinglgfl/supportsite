Review USO Accounts & Nominated Contacts

The following pages are designed to help you keep your school's USO user accounts accurate, manage nominated contacts, ensure all users are correctly synced with your MIS, and carry out other key account management tasks.

Ensure your list of administrators and authorised contacts is up to date, and that they have the correct access to key systems.
Manage Nominated Contacts

View and manage your school's USO accounts to ensure user records are accurate and up to date. Take actions such as resetting passwords and updating details.
View User List

Ensure your MIS data is syncing correctly with USO. A successful sync reduces admin by automatically creating, updating, or removing accounts. It’s also important to check for users who require matching, as unmatched accounts may not reflect key changes such as leavers or name changes, and could result in accounts not being created at all.
Manage AutoUpdate Review Unmatched Users

Review non-MIS-linked accounts to identify those that may no longer be needed. Use details like last login and password change date to guide your decisions.
Review Non-MIS Accounts

Set and review your school’s USO account password policies to ensure accounts meet your security requirements.
Update Password Policies
