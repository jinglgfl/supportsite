Review USO Accounts & Nominated Contacts

The following pages are designed to help you keep your school's USO user accounts accurate, manage Nominated Contacts, ensure all users are correctly synced with your MIS, and carry out other key account management tasks.

Ensure your list of administrators and authorised contacts is up to date, and that they have the correct access to key systems.
Manage Nominated Contacts

View and manage your school's USO accounts to ensure user records are accurate and up to date. Take actions such as resetting passwords and updating details.
View User List

Ensure your MIS data is syncing correctly with USO. A successful sync reduces admin by automatically creating, updating, or removing accounts. It’s also important to check for users who require matching, as unmatched accounts may not reflect key changes such as leavers or name changes, and could result in accounts not being created at all.
Manage AutoUpdate Review Unmatched Users

Review non-MIS-linked accounts to identify those that may no longer be needed. Use details like last login and password change date to guide your decisions.
Review Non-MIS Accounts

Set and review your school’s USO account password policies to ensure accounts meet your security requirements.
Update Password Policies

Manage Filtering

The following pages are designed to help you manage your school's internet filtering settings, ensure age-appropriate access for all users, apply safeguarding policies effectively, and carry out other key filtering and online safety tasks.

Control and update your SchoolProtect settings, including administrator access permissions and authorized contacts who can manage your school's filtering system. This ensures that only appropriate staff members have access to modify protection settings.
Manage SchoolProtect

Configure and maintain your HomeProtect settings to ensure adequate internet safety when students access resources from home. Manage user accounts, reset passwords, and update profile details to maintain appropriate filtering levels outside of school.
Manage HomeProtect

Review your school's filtering health check (MOT) report to ensure all filtering systems are working properly. This comprehensive assessment helps identify potential gaps in your protection setup and provides recommendations for maintaining optimal filtering performance.
View Filtering MOT

Perform regular audits of your filtering system to verify that all safeguarding measures are working correctly. Run tests on blocked and allowed content, review filter categories, and ensure all user groups have appropriate access levels based on age and requirements.
Carry Out Filtering Checks

Configure user permissions and access levels to control what content different user groups can access. Set up role-based filtering profiles, customize access policies for staff and students, and implement special permissions for specific educational needs.
Manage Permissions

Cybersecurity

The following options help you maintain robust cybersecurity for your school by identifying vulnerabilities and implementing security best practices to protect your digital infrastructure and sensitive data.

Schedule a comprehensive internal network vulnerability assessment to identify potential security weaknesses within your school's infrastructure. Our security experts will scan your internal systems, devices, and applications to detect vulnerabilities that could be exploited by malicious actors, providing you with a detailed report and remediation recommendations.
Request Internal Vulnerability Scan

Obtain a comprehensive external security assessment that evaluates your school's cybersecurity posture from an outside perspective. This report includes analysis of your external-facing systems, public-facing websites, email security configurations, and potential exposure points. Receive expert recommendations for strengthening your security perimeter and protecting against emerging threats.
Request External Security Report

Arrange a comprehensive health check for your school's firewall configuration and security settings. Our experts will review your firewall rules, security policies, and traffic management settings to ensure optimal protection against network threats. The MOT includes verification of proper segmentation, rule optimization recommendations, and identification of potential security gaps in your network's first line of defense.
Request Firewall MOT

Network Configuration

The following options allow you to view and manage key network configuration settings for your school, including firewall rules, mail relay settings, IP addresses, and DNS records.

Review your current firewall configuration and mail relay settings to ensure your network security and email systems are properly configured. This page provides a comprehensive view of all active rules, including ports, protocols, and access restrictions in place for your school's network.
View Firewall & Mail Relay Rules

Access a complete listing of all IP addresses assigned to your school's network, including public and private ranges. This information is essential for network troubleshooting, setting up new services, and configuring external access to your school's resources.
View IP Addresses

Examine your school's DNS (Domain Name System) configuration, including A records, CNAME records, MX records, and other DNS entries. Proper DNS configuration is crucial for website hosting, email delivery, and other network services that rely on domain name resolution.
Review DNS Records
